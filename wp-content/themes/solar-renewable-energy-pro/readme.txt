== Description ==

Solar Renewable Energy WordPress Theme is specially designed for websites of ecology preserving movement, Green Earth organization, any sort of organic life project, animals saving fundraiser. You can also use it for grocery store, supermarket, organic shop, and online store selling products agriculture, food, gardening, landscaping, lawn, organic and health stores. This premium theme has a beautiful, sophisticated and modern design that you will love. It has responsive layout which fits in all devices. The theme is translation-ready and supports RTL layout. You get custom page layouts to help you design pages as you want.
